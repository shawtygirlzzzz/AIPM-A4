# Week 08 Reflection

**1. What planning activity helped most in monitoring progress?**
The Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) with clearly defined dependencies (Week 04) proved to be the most valuable tool. By explicitly identifying Model Training as a prerequisite for Model Evaluation, the team was able to quickly understand why Task 5.1 was delayed, despite the task owner (Hor Ying Huai) being prepared to proceed. Without this dependency mapping, responsibility for the delay could have been incorrectly assigned.
